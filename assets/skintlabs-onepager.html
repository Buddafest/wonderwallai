The Problem

Small and mid-sized online businesses are drowning in repetitive customer support — and most can't afford to hire a full support team. At the same time, AI tools designed for enterprise are too complex, too expensive, and too easy to abuse. There is no approachable, plug-and-play AI layer built specifically for independent operators.

Our Products
J
Jerry The Customer Service Bot
Shopify App Store — AI Customer Support

Jerry The Customer Service Bot is an AI customer service agent that installs on any Shopify store in minutes. He answers product, order, and shipping questions instantly, 24/7, in multiple languages, with optional voice mode powered by OpenAI.

No training required. No extra staff. Jerry reads live store data and handles the repetitive questions so store owners can focus on growing their business.

Shopify Billing API OpenAI TTS Multi-language $49/mo entry plan
W
WonderwallAi
Open Source SDK + Hosted API — AI Firewall

WonderwallAi is a 4-layer AI firewall that protects any LLM-powered product from prompt injection, jailbreaks, and off-topic abuse. Developers drop it in as an SDK or call the hosted API.

MIT licensed and open source — built to become the industry-standard safety layer for AI applications, with a paid hosted API tier for teams who don't want to self-host.
